Geneva Greyhound

The greyhound is intelligent, gentle with a quiet disposition, and in spite of its great athletic ability, is content to spend most of the day sleeping. Short on endurance it requires less exercise time than most dogs.

Funfacts:
  • Greyhounds can reach speeds up to 45mph.
  • When running, greyhounds spend 75% of their time in the air.
  • Some Greyhounds sleep with their eyes open.
